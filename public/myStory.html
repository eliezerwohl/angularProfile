My Story

Or How I Learned to Stop Worrying and Love the Code

I’ve been working on websites for many years, since the days of Homestead, Xanga, GeoCities, and Myspace.

I’ve always had a desire to do more with this hobby.

In 2015, I saw an ad for a Rutgers Coding Bootcamp, and I was immediately intrigued. I was impressed by the Javascript based full stack curriculum.

Ever since I stepped into the first class, I’ve been hooked. I’ve been coding and improving my skills every day.

Since finishing the course, I've managed to turn my hobby into a paying career and been able increases the complexity of my side project
